Project Site Plan

The History and Evolution of RPGs

WDD 231 Individual Project

1. Site Name

Site Name: The RPG Chronicles

Reasoning: I selected this name because "Chronicles" implies a historical record or narrative, which fits perfectly with the goal of documenting the evolution of the genre. It sounds adventurous yet educational, appealing to both gamers and history enthusiasts.

2. Site Purpose

Overview: This is a three-page informational website dedicated to the History and Evolution of Role-Playing Games (RPGs).

The site will focus on three main areas of RPGs across different platforms and eras:

  • Home/Overview: A brief introduction to the genre of Role-Playing Games and a navigation hub.
  • The Foundation (Tabletop & Early Consoles): This page will cover the origins of the genre, focusing on foundational examples like Dungeons & Dragons and influential early video games (e.g., 80s/90s classics). It will detail core mechanics like character progression, narrative choice, and turn-based combat.
  • The Modern Era (MMOs & Open World): This page will discuss the evolution of RPGs into large-scale online experiences and modern single-player open-world environments. It will compare design philosophies and note the impact of technology on narrative.

Technical Goals: The site will use HTML for structure, CSS for responsive design and visual presentation (including animations), and JavaScript for minor interactive elements like navigation.

3. Scenarios

The following questions represent the needs of the target audience (gamers, students, and hobbyists):

  • Scenario 1: "I love modern games like Skyrim or World of Warcraft, but I don't know where the ideas came from. How did tabletop games like D&D influence the video games I play today?"
  • Scenario 2: "I am writing a paper on game design history. What are the key differences in mechanics between early turn-based RPGs and modern open-world action RPGs?"

Body Text: Inter

Usage: Used for paragraphs, lists, and general content. Inter is a clean, highly legible font optimized for user interfaces and screens, ensuring accessibility and a professional feel.
